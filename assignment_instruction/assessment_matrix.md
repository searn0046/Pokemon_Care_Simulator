# Assessment Matrix — TA sessions

## INF-1400 Mandatory Assignment 2: The Pokémon Care Simulator

This assessment matrix describes characteristics of goal achievement for OOP learning objectives at three levels: **Above expected**, **Expected**, and **Under expected**.

The matrix is used during the 10-minute question sessions between TA's and students, discussing the application of OOP principles in the students' solution to the mandatory assignment 2.
Each student gets 1 of the 6 topics assigned randomly when they show up for their session. Each topic has 5 subtopics that will be evaluated.

- **Above expected**: Deep understanding, can discuss trade-offs and alternatives.
- **Expected**: Just enough to pass. Can explain basic choices and demonstrate conscious design.
- **Under expected**: Just below passing. Anything at or below this description is evaluated as "under expected".

The guideline for passing is obtaining 4 of 5 subtopics at level "Expected" or higher.
